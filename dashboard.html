Welcome , to PlatePal

-What Is PlatePal?-

PlatePal is an easy to use shopping companion. It serves as a platform to publish personal cooking recipes to share with others. PlatePal assists the user with their shopping and calorie management. PlatePal acts as a one-stop website for all food lovers and gives them access to many different tools.

-Key Features of PlatePal-

-AI Assisted Recipe Creation

-Recipe Sharing

-Smart Shopping List

-Calorie Management

-Daily Meal Planning

-How To Use It-

-Recipes can be created within the recipe library page. You can either upload an image or manually enter the details.

-Public recipes can be viewed on the public library page. You can also add other users' recipes to your own library!

-On the smart list page, you can create a shopping list after choosing recipes from our library that you want to buy.

-You can plan out your daily meals and calculate the calories on the calorie calculator page.
